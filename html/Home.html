Our values

Equal rights and opportunities. We want everyone to have the same chances of make a project reality, the same rights of accessing funds, away from the prejudice of their origin, background, size, gender, race, identity, religion, ethnicity, location, region, area of operation, age or year of establishment. As long as you have an idea that if implemented will have a positive impact in your community, we are there to help you make it happen.

Transparency and accountability

We guarantee high standards of integrity, honesty and respect. We treat everyone with respect, the same way we want to be treated.

Responsibility and commitment.

We believe that everyone deserves opportunities to make an impact to their community. We help you make it possible.



Funding equality is a team of experts helping not-for-profits, community groups, and activists working on climate change, security and gender equality. Why do we exist? We exist because not-for-profits, community groups, and individual activists, all around the wold don’t have enough employed staff, or time to spend in doing research, writing, and budgeting project proposals. Where do we work? We are based in England, but we offer our service everywhere where there are not-for-profits, community groups, and individual activists that need our help in making an impact by assisting in writing, budgeting a project proposal while applying for funding and/or grants. Who do we serve? We serve not-for-profits, community groups, and individual activists - regardless of their origin, background, size, gender, race, identity, religion, ethnicity, location, region, area of operation, age or year of establishment – that want to apply for funding and/or grants in the fields of environment and climate change, security and peacebuilding, gender equality and youth empowerment. What sets us apart? Our staff is comprised by academia and experts working in the fields of environment and climate change, security and peacebuilding, gender equality and youth empowerment, with international experience of up to 25 years. We have experience with donors such as public agencies in individual European countries, USA, the European Union, global and national not-for-profits, as well as private donors. We currently operate in three languages, English, French, and Italian.
